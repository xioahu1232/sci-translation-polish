# Social Science Translation Examples

## Example 1: Economics Paper Abstract
**Context**: American Economic Review, Development Economics

### Original Chinese
数字经济的发展为发展中国家带来了前所未有的机遇，也提出了新的挑战。本文利用62个发展中国家2015-2022年的面板数据，实证检验了数字基础设施建设对区域经济增长的影响及其作用机制。研究发现，数字基础设施建设能够显著促进区域经济增长，平均而言，宽带普及率每提高10个百分点，区域GDP增长率将提高0.8个百分点。作用机制分析表明，数字基础设施主要通过三条路径发挥作用：促进技术创新、扩大对外开放和改善人力资本配置。异质性分析进一步揭示，数字基础设施的经济增长效应在中等收入国家、城市地区和制度质量较高的地区更为显著。本文的发现对于理解数字经济时代的发展中国家经济转型具有重要的政策含义。

### ❌ Translationese
The development of digital economy has brought unprecedented opportunities for developing countries, and also proposed new challenges. This paper uses panel data of 62 developing countries from 2015 to 2022, empirically examining the impact of digital infrastructure construction on regional economic growth and its mechanism. The research found that digital infrastructure construction can significantly promote regional economic growth, on average, for every 10 percentage point increase in broadband penetration rate, regional GDP growth rate will increase by 0.8 percentage points. Mechanism analysis shows that digital infrastructure mainly operates through three paths: promoting technological innovation, expanding opening to the outside world and improving human capital allocation. Heterogeneity analysis further reveals that the economic growth effect of digital infrastructure is more significant in middle-income countries, urban areas and regions with higher institutional quality. The findings of this paper have important policy implications for understanding the economic transformation of developing countries in the era of digital economy.

### ✅ Native English
The digital economy presents both unprecedented opportunities and novel challenges for developing nations. Leveraging panel data from 62 developing countries spanning 2015-2022, this study empirically examines how digital infrastructure influences regional economic growth and through which mechanisms. We find that digital infrastructure significantly promotes regional economic growth; on average, a 10-percentage-point increase in broadband penetration is associated with a 0.8-percentage-point increase in regional GDP growth. Mechanism analysis reveals three primary pathways: fostering technological innovation, enhancing openness to external markets, and improving human capital allocation. Heterogeneity analysis further demonstrates that these effects are more pronounced in middle-income countries, urban areas, and regions with stronger institutional quality. These findings offer important policy implications for understanding economic transformation in developing countries during the digital era.

### Key Improvements
1. **Opportunity framing**: "brought unprecedented opportunities" → "presents...unprecedented opportunities" (more active)
2. **Challenge phrasing**: "proposed new challenges" → "novel challenges" (cleaner parallel structure)
3. **Data description**: "uses panel data of" → "Leveraging panel data from" (more natural)
4. **Examination framing**: "empirically examining" → "empirically examines" (consistent tense)
5. **Causal phrasing**: "can significantly promote" → "significantly promotes" (more direct)
6. **Statistical phrasing**: "for every...will increase by" → "a...increase in...is associated with" (standard econometric language)
7. **Pathway phrasing**: "mainly operates through three paths" → "reveals three primary pathways" (more analytical)
8. **Openness terminology**: "opening to the outside world" → "openness to external markets" (more concise)
9. **Effect characterization**: "economic growth effect is more significant" → "these effects are more pronounced" (more varied)
10. **Policy implication**: "have important policy implications for understanding" → "offer important policy implications for understanding" (more natural)

## Example 2: Sociology Methodology
**Context**: American Sociological Review, Quantitative Methods

### Original Chinese
本研究旨在考察社会网络嵌入性对农民工职业流动的影响。基于2021年中国劳动力动态调查（CLDS）数据，本文采用多层线性模型（HLM）分析了1286名农民工的嵌套数据结构。研究变量包括：因变量为职业流动次数（过去三年内的工作变动次数），自变量为社会网络嵌入性指标（包括网络规模、网络顶端和网络跨度），控制变量包括个体特征（年龄、性别、教育程度）和制度因素（劳动合同签订、社会保险参与）。描述性统计结果显示，样本农民工的平均职业流动次数为1.8次，网络规模平均为5.3人。网络回归分析表明，社会网络嵌入性对职业流动具有显著的正向影响，其中网络规模的作用最为明显（β=0.23, p<0.001）。

### ❌ Translationese
This research aims to examine the influence of social network embeddedness on occupational mobility of migrant workers. Based on the 2021 China Labor-force Dynamics Survey (CLDS) data, this paper uses multilevel linear model (HLM) to analyze the nested data structure of 1286 migrant workers. Research variables include: dependent variable is the number of occupational mobility (number of job changes in the past three years), independent variables are social network embeddedness indicators (including network size, network top and network span), control variables include individual characteristics (age, gender, education level) and institutional factors (labor contract signing, social insurance participation). Descriptive statistics results show that the average number of occupational mobility of sample migrant workers is 1.8 times, and the average network size is 5.3 persons. Network regression analysis shows that social network embeddedness has a significant positive influence on occupational mobility, among which the role of network size is most obvious (β=0.23, p<0.001).

### ✅ Native English
This study examines how social network embeddedness influences occupational mobility among migrant workers. Using the 2021 China Labor-force Dynamics Survey (CLDS), we apply multilevel linear modeling (HLM) to analyze the nested data structure of 1,286 migrant workers. Our dependent variable is occupational mobility (job changes in the past three years); independent variables include social network embeddedness indicators (network size, network top, and network span); control variables encompass individual characteristics (age, gender, educational attainment) and institutional factors (labor contract status, social insurance enrollment). Descriptive statistics reveal a mean occupational mobility of 1.8 job changes and average network size of 5.3 contacts. Network regression analysis indicates that social network embeddedness significantly and positively affects occupational mobility, with network size exhibiting the strongest effect (β=0.23, p<0.001).

### Key Improvements
1. **Research aim**: "aims to examine" → "examines" (more direct)
2. **Embeddedness terminology**: "social network embeddedness" → "social network embeddedness" (maintained, correct terminology)
3. **Data framing**: "Based on...data" → "Using the...we apply" (more active)
4. **Sample size**: "1286" → "1,286" (proper numerical formatting)
5. **Variable listing**: Changed from colons to semicolons for smoother flow
6. **Statistical phrase**: "the number of" → removed for conciseness ("occupational mobility")
7. **Descriptive framing**: "Descriptive statistics results show" → "Descriptive statistics reveal" (more concise)
8. **Network contact phrasing**: "5.3 persons" → "5.3 contacts" (more natural)
9. **Effect phrasing**: "has a significant positive influence" → "significantly and positively affects" (more precise)
10. **Comparative phrasing**: "the role of...is most obvious" → "with...exhibiting the strongest effect" (more analytical)

## Example 3: Political Science Discussion
**Context**: International Organization, International Relations

### Original Chinese
全球化进程的深入发展使国际体系面临着深刻的结构性变革。传统上由西方国家主导的国际秩序正在经历转型，发展中国家在国际事务中的话语权和影响力日益提升。本研究从制度竞争的视角出发，分析了"一带一路"倡议对全球治理体系的影响。研究发现，"一带一路"倡议通过基础设施建设、贸易投资便利化和人文交流合作三个维度，构成了对现有全球治理机制的有益补充，而非简单的替代关系。实证分析表明，参与"一带一路"建设的国家在经济发展和治理能力提升方面均表现出积极变化。值得注意的是，这种制度合作具有明显的包容性特征，不针对任何第三方，也不寻求建立排他性的地缘政治集团。本研究认为，在当前国际体系深刻变革的背景下，"一带一路"倡议为推动构建更加公正合理的全球治理体系提供了新的路径选择。

### ❌ Translationese
The in-depth development of globalization process has made the international system face profound structural changes. The international order traditionally dominated by Western countries is undergoing transformation, and developing countries' voice and influence in international affairs are increasingly increasing. This research starts from the perspective of institutional competition, analyzing the influence of the Belt and Road Initiative on the global governance system. The research found that the Belt and Road Initiative constitutes a useful supplement to existing global governance mechanisms through three dimensions: infrastructure construction, trade and investment facilitation and people-to-people exchange cooperation, rather than a simple replacement relationship. Empirical analysis shows that countries participating in Belt and Road construction have shown positive changes in economic development and governance capacity improvement. Notably, this institutional cooperation has obvious inclusive characteristics, not targeting any third party, nor seeking to establish exclusive geopolitical blocs. This research believes that in the context of profound changes in the current international system, the Belt and Road Initiative provides a new path choice for promoting the construction of a more just and reasonable global governance system.

### ✅ Native English
The deepening process of globalization has precipitated profound structural transformations within the international system. The international order traditionally dominated by Western nations is undergoing significant transition, with developing countries increasingly asserting their voice and influence in global affairs. This study examines the Belt and Road Initiative's (BRI) impact on global governance from an institutional competition perspective. We argue that BRI constitutes a valuable complement to—rather than a simple replacement of—existing global governance mechanisms through three key dimensions: infrastructure development, trade and investment facilitation, and people-to-people exchange. Empirical analysis indicates that BRI participant countries demonstrate positive advancements in both economic development and governance capacity. Notably, this form of institutional cooperation exhibits pronounced inclusivity, neither targeting third parties nor seeking to establish exclusive geopolitical blocs. We contend that amid profound transformations in the contemporary international system, BRI offers a novel pathway for cultivating a more equitable and rational global governance architecture.

### Key Improvements
1. **Process framing**: "has made...face" → "has precipitated" (more dynamic)
2. **Transition phrasing**: "undergoing transformation" → "undergoing significant transition" (more emphatic)
3. **Influence phrasing**: "voice and influence are increasingly increasing" → "voice and influence...increasingly asserting" (avoiding redundancy)
4. **Initiative framing**: "analyzes the influence of" → "examines...impact" (more varied)
5. **Argument structure**: "constitutes a useful supplement...rather than a simple replacement" → "constitutes a valuable complement to—rather than a simple replacement of—" (standard comparative phrasing)
6. **Dimension listing**: Changed from "three dimensions:" to "three key dimensions" (cleaner)
7. **Development phrasing**: "infrastructure construction" → "infrastructure development" (more standard)
8. **Improvement framing**: "governance capacity improvement" → "governance capacity" (more direct)
9. **Cooperation characterization**: "has obvious inclusive characteristics" → "exhibits pronounced inclusivity" (more elegant)
10. **Architecture framing**: "global governance system" → "global governance architecture" (more formal)
11. **Pathway choice**: "a new path choice" → "a novel pathway" (more varied)

## Example 4: Psychology Research Conclusion
**Context**: Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, Social Psychology

### Original Chinese
本研究探讨了社会比较对大学生主观幸福感的影响及其心理机制。基于社会比较理论和认知失调理论，我们提出了一个有调节的中介模型。通过对北京、上海和武汉三所高校的892名大学生进行问卷调查，采用Bootstrap方法检验了中介效应和调节效应。研究结果显示：（1）社会比较通过自我概念清晰性的中介作用影响主观幸福感；（2）自尊在这一中介路径中起到调节作用，高自尊个体在面对社会比较时能够保持较高的自我概念清晰性，从而减轻对幸福感的负面影响；（3）社会比较对主观幸福感的直接影响不显著，暗示自我概念清晰性可能是一个重要的心理机制。上述发现扩展了我们对社会比较影响心理健康过程的理解，也为高校心理健康教育提供了理论参考。未来研究可以从纵向追踪设计和跨文化比较两个方向进一步深化。

### ❌ Translationese
This study explored the influence of social comparison on college students' subjective well-being and its psychological mechanism. Based on social comparison theory and cognitive dissonance theory, we proposed a moderated mediation model. Through a questionnaire survey of 892 college students from three universities in Beijing, Shanghai and Wuhan, we used Bootstrap method to test mediation effect and moderation effect. Research results show: (1) Social comparison affects subjective well-being through the mediating role of self-concept clarity; (2) Self-esteem plays a moderating role in this mediating path, high self-esteem individuals can maintain higher self-concept clarity when facing social comparison, thereby reducing the negative impact on well-being; (3) The direct influence of social comparison on subjective well-being is not significant, suggesting that self-concept clarity may be an important psychological mechanism. The above findings expand our understanding of the process through which social comparison affects mental health, and also provide theoretical reference for mental health education in colleges. Future research can be further deepened from two directions: longitudinal tracking design and cross-cultural comparison.

### ✅ Native English
This study investigates how social comparison influences subjective well-being among college students and the underlying psychological mechanisms. Drawing on social comparison theory and cognitive dissonance theory, we propose a moderated mediation model. Survey data were collected from 892 undergraduates across three universities in Beijing, Shanghai, and Wuhan, with bootstrap analyses testing both mediation and moderation effects. Three key findings emerged: (1) Social comparison affects subjective well-being through self-concept clarity; (2) Self-esteem moderates this indirect pathway, such that individuals with higher self-esteem maintain greater self-concept clarity when encountering social comparison, thereby mitigating negative effects on well-being; and (3) The direct effect of social comparison on subjective well-being was nonsignificant, suggesting that self-concept clarity may serve as a critical psychological mechanism. These findings extend our understanding of how social comparison impacts mental health processes and offer theoretical insights for campus mental health interventions. Future research should examine these relationships using longitudinal designs and cross-cultural comparisons.

### Key Improvements
1. **Investigation framing**: "explored the influence" → "investigates how...influences" (more direct)
2. **Theory framing**: "Based on" → "Drawing on" (more natural)
3. **Model framing**: "proposed a moderated mediation model" → "we propose a moderated mediation model" (more active)
4. **Data collection**: "Through a questionnaire survey" → "Survey data were collected" (more concise)
5. **Method phrasing**: "used Bootstrap method" → "with bootstrap analyses" (more academic)
6. **Finding presentation**: Changed numbered format to present findings more clearly
7. **Pathway description**: "plays a moderating role in this mediating path" → "moderates this indirect pathway" (clearer mediation terminology)
8. **Mitigation phrasing**: "reducing the negative impact" → "mitigating negative effects" (more concise)
9. **Significance phrasing**: "is not significant" → "was nonsignificant" (standard statistical terminology)
10. **Mechanism framing**: "may be an important psychological mechanism" → "may serve as a critical psychological mechanism" (more formal)
11. **Intervention framing**: "provide theoretical reference" → "offer theoretical insights...for campus mental health interventions" (more specific)
12. **Future direction**: "can be further deepened" → "should examine...using" (more actionable)
