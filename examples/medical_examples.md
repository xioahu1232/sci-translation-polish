# Medical Translation Examples

## Example 1: Clinical Trial Abstract
**Context**: Nature Medicine, Oncology

### Original Chinese
本研究采用随机双盲对照试验设计，共纳入240名晚期肺癌患者。患者被随机分为治疗组和对照组，治疗组接受靶向药物治疗，对照组接受标准化疗。主要终点为无进展生存期（PFS），次要终点包括总生存期（OS）、客观缓解率（ORR）和安全性。结果显示，治疗组的无进展生存期显著优于对照组（中位PFS: 12.6个月 vs 6.3个月，HR=0.52, P<0.001）。亚组分析表明，EGFR突变阳性的患者获益更为明显（HR=0.38, P<0.0001）。治疗期间，治疗组最常见的3-4级不良反应为皮疹（15.4%）和腹泻（12.8%），且均可控。本研究证实了该靶向药物在晚期肺癌患者中的显著疗效和良好的安全性。

### ❌ Translationese
This study used a randomized double-blind controlled trial design, a total of 240 advanced lung cancer patients were included. Patients were randomly divided into treatment group and control group, the treatment group received targeted drug treatment, the control group received standard chemotherapy. The primary endpoint was progression-free survival (PFS), secondary endpoints included overall survival (OS), objective response rate (ORR) and safety. Results showed that the progression-free survival of the treatment group was significantly better than the control group (median PFS: 12.6 months vs 6.3 months, HR=0.52, P<0.001). Subgroup analysis showed that EGFR mutation positive patients benefited more obviously (HR=0.38, P<0.0001). During the treatment period, the treatment group's most common 3-4 grade adverse reactions were rash (15.4%) and diarrhea (12.8%), and both could be controlled. This study confirmed the significant efficacy and good safety of this targeted drug in advanced lung cancer patients.

### ✅ Native English
We conducted a randomized, double-blind, controlled trial enrolling 240 patients with advanced lung cancer. Participants were randomly assigned to receive either targeted therapy (n=120) or standard chemotherapy (n=120). The primary endpoint was progression-free survival (PFS); secondary endpoints included overall survival (OS), objective response rate (ORR), and safety. The targeted therapy group demonstrated significantly superior PFS compared with the control group (median PFS: 12.6 months vs 6.3 months; HR=0.52, P<0.001). Subgroup analyses revealed enhanced benefits among patients with EGFR-positive mutations (HR=0.38, P<0.0001). The most frequent grade 3-4 adverse events in the treatment group were rash (15.4%) and diarrhea (12.8%), both of which were manageable. These findings demonstrate the substantial efficacy and favorable safety profile of targeted therapy in advanced lung cancer.

### Key Improvements
1. **Sentence structure**: Changed "a total of 240...were included" to active voice "We conducted...enrolling 240 patients"
2. **Group assignment**: Replaced passive "were divided into...received" with clear n designation: "assigned to receive either... (n=120)"
3. **Verb strength**: "confirmed" → "demonstrate" (more precise for clinical evidence)
4. **Adjective precision**: "good safety" → "favorable safety profile" (standard clinical terminology)
5. **Semicolon usage**: Replaced comma splice with semicolon between "PFS" and "secondary endpoints"
6. **Comparison clarity**: "significantly better than" → "superior compared with" (more formal)

## Example 2: Methodology Section
**Context**: The Lancet, Cardiology

### Original Chinese
所有患者均签署知情同意书，研究方案经医院伦理委员会批准（批准号：IRB-2023-0156）。纳入标准为：年龄18-75岁，纽约心脏学会（NYHA）心功能分级II-III级，左心室射血分数（LVEF）≤40%。排除标准包括：近期（6个月内）发生过心肌梗死，严重肾功能不全（eGFR<30 ml/min/1.73m²），或对研究药物过敏者。超声心动图检查采用Philips IE33设备，探头频率为2.5 MHz。所有检查由两名经验丰富的心脏超声医师独立进行，测量数据取三次测量的平均值。统计分析采用SPSS 25.0软件，连续变量以均数±标准差表示，组间比较采用t检验；分类变量以频数（百分比）表示，采用卡方检验。P<0.05为差异有统计学意义。

### ❌ Translationese
All patients signed informed consent forms, the study protocol was approved by the hospital ethics committee (approval number: IRB-2023-0156). Inclusion criteria were: age 18-75 years, New York Heart Association (NYHA) cardiac function class II-III, left ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) ≤40%. Exclusion criteria included: recent (within 6 months) occurrence of myocardial infarction, severe renal insufficiency (eGFR<30 ml/min/1.73m²), or allergy to study drugs. Echocardiography examination used Philips IE33 equipment, probe frequency was 2.5 MHz. All examinations were independently performed by two experienced cardiac ultrasound physicians, measurement data took the average value of three measurements. Statistical analysis used SPSS 25.0 software, continuous variables were expressed as mean±standard deviation, comparison between groups used t-test; categorical variables were expressed as frequency (percentage), used chi-square test. P<0.05 was considered as statistically significant difference.

### ✅ Native English
All participants provided written informed consent. The study protocol was approved by the Institutional Review Board of [Hospital Name] (approval number: IRB-2023-0156). Inclusion criteria were age 18-75 years, New York Heart Association (NYHA) functional class II-III, and left ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) ≤40%. Exclusion criteria included myocardial infarction within the previous 6 months, severe renal impairment (eGFR<30 mL/min/1.73m²), or known allergy to study medications. Echocardiographic examinations were performed using a Philips IE33 system with a 2.5 MHz transducer. Two experienced echocardiographers conducted all examinations independently; values reported represent the mean of three consecutive measurements. Statistical analyses were performed using SPSS version 25.0. Continuous variables are presented as mean±SD, and between-group comparisons were made using Student's t-test. Categorical variables are reported as counts (percentages) and analyzed with the chi-square test. Statistical significance was set at P<0.05.

### Key Improvements
1. **Patient terminology**: "All patients" → "All participants" (more inclusive language)
2. **Ethical phrasing**: "signed informed consent forms" → "provided written informed consent" (standard ethical wording)
3. **Metric precision**: "cardiac function class" → "functional class" (correct NYHA terminology)
4. **Medical terminology**: "renal insufficiency" → "renal impairment" (more precise)
5. **Instrument description**: "used Philips IE33 equipment" → "were performed using a Philips IE33 system"
6. **Data reporting**: "took the average value" → "represent the mean of" (more professional)
7. **Statistical phrasing**: "was considered as statistically significant difference" → "statistical significance was set at P<0.05" (concise)
8. **Version specification**: "SPSS 25.0 software" → "SPSS version 25.0" (standard format)

## Example 3: Discussion Section
**Context**: Journal of Clinical Investigation, Immunology

### Original Chinese
本研究首次发现了炎症小体NLRP3在重症急性胰腺炎（SAP）发病机制中的关键作用。与既往研究不同，我们不仅观察到了NLRP3在胰腺组织中的表达上调，还通过动物模型证实了其激活与病情严重程度呈正相关。有趣的是，我们发现在使用MCC950特异性抑制NLRP3后，胰腺组织病理学损伤显著减轻，血清炎症因子水平明显下降。这一发现为SAP的临床治疗提供了新的靶点。然而，本研究也存在一些局限性。首先，样本量相对较小，可能影响结果的普适性；其次，动物模型虽能模拟人类SAP的某些特征，但无法完全再现其复杂性；最后，NLRP3激活的具体分子机制尚未完全阐明。未来的研究需要进一步探讨NLRP3上下游信号通路，并开展大规模临床试验验证其治疗价值。

### ❌ Translationese
This study for the first time discovered the key role of inflammasome NLRP3 in the pathogenesis of severe acute pancreatitis (SAP). Different from previous studies, we not only observed the up-regulation of NLRP3 expression in pancreatic tissue, but also through animal models confirmed that its activation was positively correlated with disease severity. Interestingly, we found that after using MCC950 to specifically inhibit NLRP3, pancreatic tissue pathological injury was significantly alleviated, serum inflammatory cytokine levels were significantly decreased. This discovery provided a new target for the clinical treatment of SAP. However, this study also has some limitations. First, the sample size was relatively small, possibly affecting the universality of the results; second, although animal models could simulate some characteristics of human SAP, they could not completely reproduce its complexity; finally, the specific molecular mechanism of NLRP3 activation has not been fully elucidated. Future studies need to further explore the upstream and downstream signal pathways of NLRP3, and carry out large-scale clinical trials to verify its therapeutic value.

### ✅ Native English
This study is the first to demonstrate a critical role for the NLRP3 inflammasome in the pathogenesis of severe acute pancreatitis (SAP). Unlike previous investigations, we not only observed NLRP3 upregulation in pancreatic tissue but also established, using animal models, a positive correlation between NLRP3 activation and disease severity. Notably, pharmacological inhibition of NLRP3 with MCC950 significantly reduced pancreatic histopathological injury and markedly decreased circulating inflammatory cytokine levels. These findings identify NLRP3 as a potential therapeutic target for SAP. Several limitations warrant consideration. First, the relatively small sample size may limit the generalizability of our findings. Second, while animal models recapitulate certain features of human SAP, they cannot fully reproduce its complexity. Third, the precise molecular mechanisms underlying NLRP3 activation remain to be fully elucidated. Future investigations should explore the upstream and downstream signaling pathways of NLRP3 and evaluate its therapeutic potential in large-scale clinical trials.

### Key Improvements
1. **Opening strength**: "for the first time discovered" → "is the first to demonstrate" (more formal academic voice)
2. **Verb precision**: "confirmed that" → "established" (stronger academic verb)
3. **Causal clarity**: "after using...inhibit" → "pharmacological inhibition of NLRP3 with..." (more precise)
4. **Tissue description**: "pathological injury was significantly alleviated" → "reduced...histopathological injury" (standard pathology terminology)
5. **Medical precision**: "serum inflammatory cytokine" → "circulating inflammatory cytokine" (more specific)
6. **Transition improvement**: "However, this study also has some limitations" → "Several limitations warrant consideration" (smoother transition)
7. **Limitation framing**: "affecting the universality" → "limit the generalizability" (correct terminology)
8. **Model comparison**: "simulate...reproduce" → "recapitulate...reproduce" (more precise verb choice)
9. **Future research phrasing**: "need to further explore" → "should explore" (more direct academic voice)

## Example 4: Conclusion Section
**Context**: NEJM, Epidemiology

### Original Chinese
综上所述，本研究证实了维生素D缺乏与2型糖尿病发病风险之间存在显著关联。我们的研究表明，血清25(OH)D水平每降低10 ng/mL，2型糖尿病发病风险增加18%。这一发现具有重要的公共卫生意义，因为维生素D缺乏在全球范围内普遍存在。然而，需要强调的是，本研究为观察性研究设计，虽然我们进行了充分的混杂因素调整，但仍无法排除残余混杂的可能性。因此，维生素D补充是否能降低2型糖尿病发病风险，仍需通过大规模随机对照试验进一步验证。尽管如此，本研究为理解维生素D在糖代谢中的作用机制提供了重要线索，也为未来的干预研究奠定了基础。

### ❌ Translationese
In summary, this study confirmed that there is a significant association between vitamin D deficiency and the risk of type 2 diabetes onset. Our research showed that for every 10 ng/mL decrease in serum 25(OH)D level, type 2 diabetes onset risk increased by 18%. This finding has important public health significance, because vitamin D deficiency is universally present globally. However, it needs to be emphasized that this study was an observational study design, although we conducted sufficient confounding factor adjustment, still could not exclude the possibility of residual confounding. Therefore, whether vitamin D supplementation can reduce type 2 diabetes onset risk still needs to be further verified through large-scale randomized controlled trials. Nevertheless, this study provided important clues for understanding the mechanism of vitamin D in glucose metabolism, and also laid the foundation for future intervention studies.

### ✅ Native English
In conclusion, this study demonstrates a significant association between vitamin D deficiency and risk of type 2 diabetes. Our findings indicate that each 10 ng/mL decrement in serum 25(OH)D is associated with an 18% increased risk of type 2 diabetes. This observation has substantial public health implications given the global prevalence of vitamin D deficiency. However, several limitations merit emphasis. The observational design of this study precludes causal inference, despite extensive adjustment for potential confounders; residual confounding cannot be excluded. Consequently, whether vitamin D supplementation reduces type 2 diabetes risk remains to be established in large-scale randomized controlled trials. Nonetheless, these findings provide important insights into vitamin D's role in glucose metabolism and lay a foundation for future intervention studies.

### Key Improvements
1. **Conclusion wording**: "In summary" → "In conclusion" (standard section opener)
2. **Verb strength**: "confirmed that there is" → "demonstrates a significant association" (more precise)
3. **Risk phrasing**: "for every 10 ng/mL decrease...increased by 18%" → "each 10 ng/mL decrement...associated with an 18% increased risk" (more precise epidemiological language)
4. **Implication framing**: "public health significance" → "public health implications" (standard terminology)
5. **Prevalence phrasing**: "universally present globally" → "global prevalence" (more concise)
6. **Limitation introduction**: "However, it needs to be emphasized" → "several limitations merit emphasis" (more academic)
7. **Causality language**: "although we conducted...still could not exclude" → "precludes causal inference...residual confounding cannot be excluded" (standard epidemiological phrasing)
8. **Research gap**: "needs to be further verified" → "remains to be established" (standard academic phrasing)
9. **Contribution framing**: "provided important clues" → "provide important insights" (more academic)
