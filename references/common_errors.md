# 常见翻译错误汇总

> 学术论文中英翻译常见问题与改正

---

## 一、词汇层面错误

### 1. "本文"翻译错误

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 说明 |
|---------|---------|------|
| This paper | This study / We | "paper"是载体，"study"是研究 |
| This article | This study | 同上 |

**例句**：
- ❌ This paper proposes a new method...
- ✅ This study proposes a new method... / We propose a new method...

---

### 2. "大量"翻译错误

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 使用场景 |
|---------|---------|----------|
| a large number of | numerous | 可数名词 |
| a lot of | extensive / substantial | 学术写作避免 |
| many | a considerable number of | 强调数量时 |

---

### 3. "重要"翻译错误

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 细微差别 |
|---------|---------|----------|
| important | crucial | 决定性的 |
| important | significant | 有统计学意义的 |
| important | key | 关键的 |
| important | essential | 必不可少的 |

---

### 4. "通过"翻译错误

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 | 使用场景 |
|---------|---------|----------|
| through | via | 方法、手段 |
| through | by | 动作执行者 |
| through | using | 工具、技术 |

**例句**：
- ❌ We improved the accuracy through the new algorithm.
- ✅ We improved accuracy via the new algorithm. / We improved accuracy using the new algorithm.

---

## 二、句式层面错误

### 1. "研究表明"句式

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| Research shows that... | Evidence suggests that... |
| Studies show that... | Findings indicate that... |
| The research proves... | The results demonstrate... |

**原因**：学术写作讲究严谨，避免过于绝对。

---

### 2. "取得...成果"句式

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| achieved good results | demonstrated promising results |
| obtained the following conclusions | reached the following conclusions |
| made great progress | made significant progress |

---

### 3. "非常/极其"句式

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| very important | highly significant |
| very large | substantially larger |
| very different | markedly different |
| extremely useful | invaluable / essential |

---

## 三、结构层面错误

### 1. 中式流水句

**错误模式**：把中文的逗号连接直接翻译成英文

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| We collected the data, analyzed the results, and found that... | We collected and analyzed the data. Our findings indicate that... |
| The method is simple, easy to implement, and has high accuracy. | The method is simple and easy to implement, with high accuracy. |

**原则**：英文每个句子一个核心意思，不要一个句子说太多。

---

### 2. 被动语态过度使用

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| It is found that... | We found that... (Nature风格) |
| The data were collected... | We collected data... |

**原则**：Nature/Science偏好主动语态，IEEE/工程领域可接受被动。

---

### 3. 名词化过度

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| made an improvement | improved |
| conducted an analysis | analyzed |
| performed a comparison | compared |

**原则**：用动词而不是名词，更直接有力。

---

## 四、逻辑层面错误

### 1. 缺少过渡

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| (两句独立陈述) | First, ... Moreover, ... |
| A happened. B happened. | A happened, which led to B. |

---

### 2. 归纳式论述（中文习惯）

| ❌ 错误（中文式） | ✅ 正确（英文式） |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| 背景 → 现象 → 分析 → 结论 | 结论 → 证据 → 解释 → 过渡 |

**原则**：英文学术写作偏好演绎式（先说结论），不是归纳式。

---

## 五、常见Chinglish表达

### 1. "make + 名词" 滥用

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| make an improvement | improve |
| make a comparison | compare |
| make an investigation | investigate |

---

### 2. "pay attention to" 滥用

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| pay attention to | focus on / emphasize |
| We should pay attention to... | Particular attention should be paid to... |

---

### 3. "have" 滥用

| ❌ 错误 | ✅ 正确 |
|---------|---------|
| have a discussion | discuss |
| have a look at | examine |
| have an effect on | affect |

---

## 六、检查清单

完成翻译后，检查以下问题：

- [ ] 没有"this paper" → 用"this study"或"we"
- [ ] 没有"a large number of" → 用"numerous"或"extensive"
- [ ] 没有"through"表示方法 → 用"via"或"using"
- [ ] 没有"research shows" → 用"evidence suggests"
- [ ] 没有"very + 形容词" → 用具体程度词
- [ ] 没有超过35词的长句 → 拆分成两句
- [ ] 没有缺少过渡的段落 → 添加过渡词
- [ ] 没有过度名词化 → 改用动词
